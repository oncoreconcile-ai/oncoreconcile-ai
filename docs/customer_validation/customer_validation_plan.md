# Customer Validation Plan

## Goal

Validate that oncology data reconciliation is a real workflow problem and that users value explainability, evidence, confidence, and review recommendations.

---

## Target Interviewees

- Molecular diagnostics laboratory director
- Clinical genomics expert
- Oncology data engineer
- GA4GH/CAT-VRS or standards expert
- Translational research scientist

---

## Interview Questions

1. Where do messy cancer type, gene, or variant names appear in your workflow?
2. How are these inconsistencies resolved today?
3. What causes the most manual review?
4. Would canonical concepts improve cohort creation or analytics?
5. What evidence would you need to trust a reconciliation result?
6. When should a record be automatically reconciled vs sent to human review?
7. What would make this tool useful but safe?
